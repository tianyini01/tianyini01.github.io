More about me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Linguistics at The Ohio State University. Before moving into linguistics, I was trained in software engineering and mathematics, and worked for two years as an engineer.

I am multilingual. My mother tongues are Mandarin Chinese and Lower Yangtze Mandarin (淮安话). I am also fluent in Spanish and German.
